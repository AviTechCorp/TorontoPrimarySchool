Academics

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive education that prepares students for success in future academic pursuits and life. We follow the national CAPS curriculum, offering a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Departments

The school is organized into the following departments:

  • **Foundation Phase (Grades R-3):** Focuses on foundational literacy, numeracy, and life skills.
  • **Intermediate Phase (Grades 4-6):** Expands on core knowledge and introduces new subjects, promoting independence and research skills.
  • **Senior Phase (Grades 7):** Prepares students for the transition to high school, with a focus on subject-specific knowledge and critical thinking.

Subjects

Our core subjects include:

  • Home Language (English)
  • First Additional Language (Afrikaans)
  • Mathematics
  • Life Skills (including Creative Arts, Physical Education, and Social Skills)

Assessments

We use a variety of assessment methods to evaluate student progress, including continuous classroom assessments, formal tests, and projects. These assessments are designed to be fair, valid, and reliable, giving a clear picture of each student's academic standing.
