Activities

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in a holistic education that extends beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular activities are designed to help students discover new talents, develop social skills, and pursue their passions. We encourage every student to participate in at least one activity each term.

Sports

Sport plays a vital role in developing discipline, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle. Our sports program includes:

  • Soccer
  • Netball
  • Athletics
  • Cricket
  • Swimming (Seasonal)

Clubs

We offer a diverse range of clubs that cater to various interests, including:

  • Debate Club
  • Chess Club
  • Art Club
  • Environmental Club

Events

Our school calendar is filled with exciting events that bring our community together, such as:

  • Annual School Fun Run
  • Heritage Day Celebration
  • Science Fair
  • Parent-Teacher Conferences
