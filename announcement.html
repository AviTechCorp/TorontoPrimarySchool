School Closure - Public Holiday

September 2, 2025

Please be advised that Toronto Primary School will be closed on **Monday, October 7th, 2025** for a public holiday. School will resume on Tuesday, October 8th. We wish everyone a safe and enjoyable long weekend.

Annual Sports Day - Reminder

August 28, 2025

A reminder to all parents and students that our Annual Sports Day is scheduled for **Saturday, September 21st, 2025**. Please ensure your child wears the correct sports attire and has a water bottle and sunblock. We look forward to a day of fun and healthy competition!

Parent-Teacher Conference Bookings Now Open

August 20, 2025

The booking portal for the upcoming Parent-Teacher Conferences on **September 10th and 11th** is now open. Please log in to your parent portal to book a 15-minute slot with your child's teacher. We encourage all parents to attend to discuss your child's progress.
